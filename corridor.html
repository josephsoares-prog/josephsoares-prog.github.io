What Corridor Intelligence is

The corridor is the place where decisions actually move. Cabinet rooms. Pre-meeting hallways. Quiet calls before the formal vote. The space where stakes are real and the room is small.

This is the body of work for people who operate in those rooms — entrepreneurs, business leaders, and senior advisors who need to read the world before it is in the headlines. Three surfaces, one editorial voice, written from the perspective of having served in Canada's Prime Minister's Office during the 2008 global financial crisis and as Chief of Staff at the Senate of Canada during the COVID-19 pandemic.
