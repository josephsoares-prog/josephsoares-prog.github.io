Framework

The SPARK Framework

Every engagement follows a five-stage process built on deep experience managing complex transitions. Clear. Tested. Focused on results.

S
Situation
Get to the truth of where you actually are — assets, liabilities, timing, constraints, untapped value.
P
Plan
Design the path forward. Deal structure. Timeline. Stakeholder strategy. Written. Tested against reality.
A
Access
Open doors. Market connections. Buyer/seller networks. Lender relationships. The infrastructure that moves deals.
R
Run
Manage the active phase. Keep stakeholders aligned. Remove obstacles. Make decisions when they matter.
K
Know
Close and debrief. Document lessons. Ensure what you built — and what comes next — is protected.
