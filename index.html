How I Work

Three tracks. One outcome: clarity and execution.

01
The SPARK Framework

SPARK Advisory Retainer

You didn't build what you've built by following a playbook. You built it through instinct, relationships, and decisions made under pressure. But the higher the stakes get, the fewer people around you can see what you see — and the loneliness of command becomes a strategic liability. I'm not a consultant who sells advice. I'm a trusted strategic partner who has been in the room when the stakes were national — serving in the Prime Minister's Office during a financial crisis and at the Senate of Canada during a pandemic. When you retain me, you get a confidential, direct-access relationship with someone who understands the weight of what you carry.

For: Self-made entrepreneurs who've outgrown their inner circle • Business leaders carrying decisions that affect significant capital, teams, or legacy • High-net-worth individuals who value directness, credibility, and results
02
Government & Stakeholder Navigation

The Bridge

Business leaders rely on economic logic. Government follows political logic. The disconnect costs millions. I've operated on both sides — inside Canada's Prime Minister's Office during the 2008 global financial crisis and at the Senate of Canada during the 2020 pandemic. I don't lobby. I facilitate understanding, and that transforms adversaries into partners. My clients avoid unnecessary conflict and achieve better outcomes.

For: Business owners running projects that collide with government or regulatory bodies • Investors and developers navigating approvals, permits, and institutional stakeholders • Leaders who have already lost money fighting the system and need a trusted operator on both sides
