This Cookie Policy explains how Joseph Soares / IBPROM Corp. ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") uses cookies and similar technologies on our website located at josephsoares.com (the "Website"). We are committed to being transparent about our data practices and respecting your privacy.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to website owners.

Similar technologies include web beacons, pixels, and local storage, which perform similar functions.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

1. Analytics and Performance

We use Google Analytics (GA4) to understand how visitors interact with our Website. This helps us improve content, functionality, and user experience. Google Analytics collects data about your interactions anonymously and does not identify you personally.

2. Essential Website Functions

We may use cookies to remember your language preference, maintain session information, and ensure the Website functions properly.

3. Embedded Forms and Content

We may embed content from third-party services (such as Substack) that may set their own cookies. These are governed by those providers' privacy policies.

What We Do NOT Use Cookies For

  • Advertising or marketing tracking across other websites
  • Social media tracking pixels or social network monitoring
  • Behavioral retargeting or cross-site profiling

How to Control Cookies

Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through settings. You can typically:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Accept cookies only from specified websites
  • Reject all cookies
  • Delete cookies when you close your browser

To manage cookies in popular browsers, visit:

  • Chrome: google.com/chrome/privacy
  • Firefox: mozilla.org/firefox/privacy
  • Safari: apple.com/safari/privacy
  • Edge: microsoft.com/edge/privacy

Google Analytics Opt-Out

If you prefer not to have your data collected by Google Analytics, you can install the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on.

Third-Party Cookie Management

You can opt out of third-party cookies via:

  • Digital Advertising Alliance (DAA): aboutads.info/choices
  • Network Advertising Initiative (NAI): optout.networkadvertising.org
