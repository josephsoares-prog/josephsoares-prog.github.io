The Six Questions Every Guest Answers

Every episode follows the same format. No surprises. These are the questions I will ask you — so you know exactly what to expect before we sit down.

01
What is the biggest decision you have ever made in your career — the one where you had the least information and the most at stake?
02
What did you know at the time that gave you the conviction to move forward when others would have waited?
03
Was there a moment you seriously considered walking away from what you built? What kept you in?
04
What is the one decision you got wrong that taught you the most about how you lead?
05
Who is the person who told you something you did not want to hear — and turned out to be right?
06
What do you know now about building something that lasts that you wish you had understood at the beginning?

These six questions are the foundation. Follow-up questions happen naturally in conversation — that is where the best moments come from.
